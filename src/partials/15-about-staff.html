Our story

A little coffee house that fell hard for books.

We started in 2012 with one wobbly espresso machine and a single shelf of paperbacks. Fourteen years later the shelves climb the walls, the back room hosts poetry by candlelight, and the espresso machine still hisses like it's in love.

Every book here has been read, argued over, and lovingly hand-noted by one of our keepers, usually over a second cup.
